Three games in which a block figure falls into a rectangular glass.

Do not let overwhelm yourself falling figures!
Wait figure and "attach" it.

In the first game (Bricks) gamer must collect a row with no spaces in the second and third (Columnus and Color balls) must collect three of the same color.

Advantages:
- Easy control
- Endless game
- Custom dimensions and shapes glass
- Adjustable speed
- The ability to rollback, save the current game state on exit
