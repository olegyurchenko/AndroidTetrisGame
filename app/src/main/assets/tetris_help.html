"Bricks" game

The game has been developed based on Tetris - commuter game created by Alexey Pajitnov in the Soviet Union (see wikipedia). The main difference - figures which represent Tetris is combination of the four squares, in the "Bricks" - combinations of squares in accordance with the level of complexity.

Gameplay

Random figures fall from above into a rectangular glass width 10 and height 20 cells. (1).

In flight, the player can rotate the figure by 90Â° and move it horizontally.

Also, you can "drop" figure, that is, to accelerate its fall, when it is decided where the figure should fall.

Figure flies until stumble on until another figure or bottom of beaker.

If at the same time filled with a horizontal row of 10 cells, it disappears and all that is above it, drops per cell.

In addition, the figure shows that will follow after the current - a hint, which allows the player to plan actions.

The game ends when a new figure can not fit into the glass.

The player receives points for each completed row, so its task - to fill in the ranks, without filling the glass itself (vertical) as long as possible, so as to get as many points as possible.
