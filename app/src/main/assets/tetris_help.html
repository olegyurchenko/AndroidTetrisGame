Tetris game collection

Tetris game
Tetris - computer game invented by Alexey Pajitnov in the Soviet Union and the public presented June 6, 1984. (Wikipedia article: wikipedia.org)

Rules

Random figures fall on top in a rectangular glass 10 wide and 20 cells high. (1).

In flight, the player can rotate the figure by 90 ° and move it horizontally.

It is also possible "to drop" a figure, that is to accelerate its falling when it is already solved where the figure shall fall.

The figure flies until comes across other figure or a glass bottom.

If at the same time a horizontal row from 10 cells was filled, it disappears also everything that above it, falls by one cell.

In addition the figure which will follow after current is shown is a hint, which allows to plan to the player of action.

A game comes to an end when the new figure can't be located in a glass.

The player receives points for each filled row therefore its task — to fill rows, without filling a glass (down) as long as possible thus to receive as much as possible points.

  1. Width and height of a glass can be changed in settings
