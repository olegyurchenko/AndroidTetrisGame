Columnus game

The idea was borrowed from the old computer DOS game.

Rules

Figures in the form of column, consisting of three squares arranged vertically one under the other, with random color, falling from the top in a rectangular glass width 10 and a height of 20 cells. (1) .

In flight, the player can change the location of the colored squares in column and move it horizontally.

It is also possible "to drop" a figure, that is to accelerate its falling when it is already solved where the figure shall fall.

The figure flies until comes across other figure or a glass bottom.

After the fall, all the squares bordering the squares of the same color vertically, horizontally and diagonally destroyed. All squares that destroyed above, falls into one cell.

A game comes to an end when the new figure can't be located in a glass.

A player gets points for every square cut, so his goal - to put column color to color without filling the glass itself (vertical) as long as possible, so as to get as many points.

  1. Width and height of a glass can be changed in settings
